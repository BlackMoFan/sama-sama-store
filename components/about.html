About Sama-Sama Store

Sama-Sama Store is a startup online shopping platform created by students from
West Visayas State University - College of ICT and Central Philippine University. It is dedicated to help students and people
to connect with products and services that can help them primarily with their educational needs. Additionally, it aims to help students
and anyone who wishes to gain extra income by selling their materials and services in this platform.
